Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I use the Mobile Booth?
To use the Mobile Booth, simply click the "Start Camera" button on the home page to activate your webcam. Once your camera is on, you can apply filters and take photos using the "Take Photo" button.
Do I need to create an account?
For the basic features, no account is required. However, to save your photos and access additional features, you'll need to sign in using the "Log In" button in the navigation menu.

Custom Backgrounds

How do I change the background of my photo?
Currently, the background changing feature is in development. In a future update, you'll be able to select from a variety of backgrounds or upload your own.
Can I upload my own background images?
Custom background upload will be available in a future update. Stay tuned!

Advanced Features

What filters are available?
Currently, we offer several filters including Grayscale, Sepia, Invert, Blur, and Brightness. We're constantly adding new filters to enhance your photo experience.
Can I share my photos directly to social media?
Social media sharing is planned for a future update. For now, you can download your photos and then upload them to your favorite social platforms.
